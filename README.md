# Sales-Dashboard

The purpose of sales dashboard is to present an overview of the sales metrics and trends in order to analyze year-over-year sales performance and understand sales trends.

# Key Requirements
Display a summary of total sales, total cost, total profit, total shipments, total boxes.

## Sales Trends
- Present the data for each KPI on a monthly basis
- Identify months sales and make them easy to recognize.
- Identify months profits and make them easy to recognize.
- Identify months costs and make them easy to recognize.

### Boxes & shipments

- How many boxes are selled and shipped compared with each month
